Bhool Bhulaiyaa 3, starring Kartik Aaryan, Vidya Balan, Triptii Dimri, unveiled its first teaser on Friday. Directed by Anees Bazmee, who also directed the second installment of the series, is back in the director’s chair for the third part. This marks Vidya’s return to the franchise after her appearance in the first installment, back in 2007. The first Bhool Bhulaiyaa starred Akshay Kumar. The teaser opens with the audio of ‘Ami Je Tomar’, and reveals the grand return of Vidya. We get a nod to the terrifying scene from the first film, where her character lifted a bed with her bare hands. This time, she lifts up a heavy chair as she screams at the top of her lungs. Kartik Aaryan is back as Rooh Baba, a ghost hunter of sorts, who appears to have been given the task of capturing Manjulika’s ghost. Triptii Dimri plays Kartik’s love interest. It was rumoured that Madhuri Dixit is also a part of the film, but we don’t see a glimpse of her in the teaser.
